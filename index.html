BTS

BTS, in full Bangtan Sonyeondan (Korean: “Bulletproof Boy Scouts” or “Bangtan Boys”), also called Beyond the Scene, South Korean K-pop (Korean pop music) band that shot to international stardom in the late 2010s. Its seven members were RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V and Jungkook. Their fan base ARMY which stands for Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth
